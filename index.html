About me

Coach Vinicius Nunes is a former Brazilian competitive swimmer with over 15 years of experience in professional swimming coaching. His journey as an athlete gives him a deep understanding of the physical and mental demands of the sport, allowing him to coach with both precision and empathy. He offers structured coaching classes for swimmers of all levels, focusing on stroke technique, efficiency, speed, and confidence in the water. By combining proven training methods with personalized attention, Coach Vinicius Nunes helps each swimmer build strong fundamentals, improve performance, and achieve long-term progress.

Highlights & Milestones

  • Diploma in Physical Education
  • Certified Lifeguard
  • Holder of two Australian swimming certifications
  • Winner of over 80 medals in competitive swimming
  • Swam alongside elite athletes, including Michael Phelps
  • 15 years of competitive swimming experience
  • 10 years of experience coaching competitive swimmers
  • Bronze medalist in the 200m Backstroke and Silver medalist in the 400m Individual Medley at the Université de Sherbrooke swimming competition

Swim Programs
swimming lessons for all age groups

Water Confidence

Builds comfort, safety, and confidence in the water through basic movement, breathing, and floating skills.

Learn to Swim

Teaches fundamental swimming skills, including breathing, floating, kicking, and basic stroke coordination.

Freestyle

Focuses on efficient front-crawl technique, breathing control, and endurance for speed and distance.

Breaststroke

Develops proper timing, glide, and technique for smooth and efficient breaststroke swimming.

Backstroke

Improves body position, arm rotation, and kick technique while swimming confidently on the back.

Butterfly

Teaches powerful dolphin kick, coordinated arm movement, and correct breathing for advanced swimmers.

IM (4 Strokes)

Trains swimmers to efficiently perform all four strokes: butterfly, backstroke, breaststroke, and freestyle.

Competitive

Prepares swimmers for competitions through advanced technique, race strategy, and performance training.

Open Water (Sea)

Develops skills for safe and confident swimming in open water, including navigation, breathing, and endurance.
